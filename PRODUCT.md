# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Companies and factories, CSR managers, sponsors and partners, educational institutions, community organizations, parents and families, and potential volunteers and leaders.

## Product Purpose
To present SSG as a trusted youth-development organization with a 40+ year legacy, and to create meaningful partnership opportunities with companies, institutions, and sponsors. The website should feel like a premium annual report, documentary, and partnership portfolio combined into one experience.

## Brand Personality
Premium, trustworthy, inspiring, professional, human-centered, community-driven, youth-development focused.

## Anti-references
SaaS dashboard aesthetics, startup landing page clichés, generic corporate templates, stock-photo-heavy designs, excessive gradients and flashy effects.

## Design Principles
1. Show impact before asking for partnership.
2. Show, don't tell (visual storytelling).
3. Real people, real experiences, real stories.
4. Premium craftsmanship and attention to detail.
5. Trust and credibility through structure and presentation.
6. Emotional storytelling supported by measurable impact.
7. Every section should feel purposeful and immersive.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Standard HTML semantics, reduced motion fallbacks where necessary, strong readable contrasts, and cross-device responsiveness.
