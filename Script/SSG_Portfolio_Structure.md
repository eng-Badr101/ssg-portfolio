# 2. About SSG

## Who We Are

Founded in **1977**, Al-Salam Scouting Groups has been dedicated to developing young people through leadership, teamwork, discipline, service, and character-building experiences.

## Our Mission
To prepare generations of young people who are disciplined, helpful, confident, and ready to serve their community through scouting values, practical experiences, and leadership development.

## Our Vision
To become a trusted youth-development platform that connects scouting, education, community service, and real-world exposure to help young people discover their potential and contribute positively to society.
